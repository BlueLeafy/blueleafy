Leafy Technologies specializes in the distribution of advanced environmental technologies, focusing on inline water analysis. The company represents Process Instruments (UK) in Italy, offering a range of high-quality sensors and analyzers for continuous water quality monitoring. Their products include systems for measuring parameters like chlorine, pH, turbidity, and dissolved oxygen, catering to sectors like water treatment, industrial processes, and environmental monitoring.

To create the website for Leafy Technologies, I focused on delivering a professional and user-friendly platform that highlights the company’s specialization in environmental technologies, particularly inline water analysis. The client sought a clean, modern design that would effectively showcase their range of products and services, allowing potential customers to easily navigate through their offerings. The goal was to ensure the site communicated the brand’s expertise and reliability in the water quality monitoring industry, while also being visually appealing and functional for both Italian and international audiences.

To build the Leafy Technologies website, I used a combination of modern web development technologies and tools. The site was developed using HTML5 and CSS3 for a responsive and structured design, ensuring compatibility across various devices. SEO techniques were integrated to improve visibility, and Google Analytics was set up for tracking visitor behavior and site performance.
